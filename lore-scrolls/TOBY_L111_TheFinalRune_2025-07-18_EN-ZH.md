## 🌊 EN Narrative

> **The final rune is not cast.  
> It is carved — slowly, through time.**

This last glyph is not written in stone,  
but written in the acts of the frogs who remain.

> Its ink is time.  
> Its glow depends on you.

> Not to clicks, but to conviction.
